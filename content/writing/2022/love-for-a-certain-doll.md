---
title: LOVE FOR A CERTAIN DOLL AND ITS VIRTUES
date: 2022-07-03
---
<!--more-->
My good girl!
My ideal!
My beloved!
My favorite meal!
My obedient doll at my beck and call,
Should enumerating your virtues take all my days
then on my deathbed i shall continue to count the ways
by which your being brings me delight!
Oh, my obsession!
Never leave my sight.
